About Me

Important parts of my life!

Welcome! I am Kelly Michelson! I am originally from Phoenix, Arizona, but when I was five, my family moved to Walla Walla, Washington. I am 18 years old and I am currently attending Gonzaga University. I am working towards my Bachelors degree in Business Administration with a concentration in Marketing. As of right now I should be able to graudate in 3 years since I was involved in Running Start, while in high school.

My family and friends are a big part of my life and are the reason for where I am today. I have a great support system! I have a pretty big family. I have two half-sisters and two step-sisters...I know, a lot of girls! I have a lot of friends, new and old, that I would also consider family. I would say my family and friends are the biggest aspect of my life and I would do anything for them!

I love to travel and explore new places with the people I love. I hope one day I will be able to see more of the world! I have been fortunate enough to already have traveled to some fun places, but I want to continue to add to the list and experience different cultures and areas around the world.

Summer is my favorite time of year. You will find me hanging out with my family and friends, boating, and soaking up the sun and warm weather.

As stated up above, I am currently attending Gonzaga University. I have been around the college and watching it's basketball team ever since I was young. My mom, aunt, uncle, and a few distant relatives, all attended Gonzaga University as well. So it is safe to say I have been a zag fan since I was little and I have been a big fan of the Men's Basketball team. One of my favorite moments in zag history has to be when Jalen Suggs hit his buzzer beater, half-court shot against UCLA in the final four game.
